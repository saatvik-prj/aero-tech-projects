Scoping It Out: A Robotic Mars Surface Exploration Mission

Background:
The first step in understanding the mission at hand is defining the scope, which is identifying and describing what is pertinent to your project. The scope content involves:

• Defining the needs, goals (science/mission), and objectives
• Identifying stakeholders
• Developing operational concepts (Con-ops)
• Understanding the constraints
